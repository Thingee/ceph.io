Reliable and scalable storage designed for any organization

Use Ceph to transform your storage infrastructure. Ceph provides a unified storage service with object, block, and file interfaces from a single cluster built from commodity hardware components. Whether your workload runs on-prem, in the public or private cloud, or is container native, Ceph is applicable to wide range of use cases.
